MusicHub is the new go-to digital space for artists to organise, release and promote their music independently and with ease.

Our hub is powered by GEMA and was founded in 2020 in conjunction with Zebralution. Our common goal is to offer a variety of intuitive tools and solutions for musicians to get their music heard, plus connect to a broader audience, fellow musicians, and supporters.

MusicHub is a DIY platform for artists who want to release their music to the leading streaming, download, and social media platforms independently and without limits, while retaining full control over their rights. You can also use MusicHub to organise your music, add lyrics, discover promotion tools, partner offers, and numerous e-learning materials.
